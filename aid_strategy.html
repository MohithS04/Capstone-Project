Global Aid Distribution

Our aid allocation strategy is based on a needs assessment that prioritizes countries facing the greatest challenges, while ensuring every nation receives appropriate support.

Aid Allocation Methodology
The allocation methodology uses weighted priority scores based on country needs. High-need countries receive a priority weight of 3, medium-need countries receive 2, and low-need countries receive 1. This ensures an equitable distribution of our $10 million aid budget.
